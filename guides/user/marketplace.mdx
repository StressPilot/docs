description: "Discover and manage Stress Pilot plugins."
---

The Marketplace is your source for extending Stress Pilot's capabilities.

## Browsing Plugins

Navigate to the **Marketplace** tab to see a list of available plugins. You can search by name or filter by tags (e.g., #web, #financial, #performance).

## Installing a Plugin

1. Click on a plugin to see its details and documentation.
2. Click **Install**.
3. Once installed, the plugin will be available when you create or edit an endpoint.

## Managing Your Plugins

In the **Marketplace** tab, go to the **My Plugins** sub-tab to see what you have installed and check for updates.
